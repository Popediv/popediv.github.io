Our Academic Structure

Grading & Assessment

Students are assessed through continuous assessments, mid-term tests, and final examinations. The grading system is as follows:

Grade Score Range Description
A 75 - 100% Excellent
B 60 - 74% Very Good
C 50 - 59% Good
D 40 - 49% Fair
F Below 40% Fail
